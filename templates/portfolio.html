About Me

Tech Innovator & Commercial Pilot

I'm Nischal Pantha, a Commercial Pilot, Founder of Namestech, and Creator of SkyBound—a comprehensive aviation platform that revolutionizes pilot training and flight operations.

My unique journey combines three interconnected passions: flying with precision, building innovative technology, and creating tools that empower the aviation community. Whether commanding an aircraft at 35,000 feet or architecting enterprise-scale systems from the ground, my philosophy remains constant: excellence through discipline, innovation through exploration, and always prioritizing safety and impact.

As a Technology Visionary

I founded Namestech to bridge the gap between ambitious business goals and cutting-edge technology solutions. My mission is to empower organizations with transformative technology that drives measurable impact. Namestech specializes in digital transformation, cloud solutions, and enterprise-grade software architecture.

As Creator of SkyBound

SkyBound represents the perfect intersection of my aviation passion and software expertise. Recognizing a critical gap in pilot training and flight operations technology, I created SkyBound—a comprehensive platform that leverages real-time flight data, weather integration, and advanced flight planning tools. SkyBound serves student pilots, flight schools, and professional aviators, improving training outcomes and operational efficiency through data-driven insights and intuitive design.

As a Commercial Pilot

Aviation represents my deepest passion—the perfect blend of technical precision, situational awareness, and continuous learning. As a commercial pilot with 1000+ flight hours, I've developed unparalleled problem-solving skills under pressure and a commitment to safety that mirrors my approach to software development. My real-world aviation experience directly informs SkyBound's design and feature development.

Our App

Skybound - Pilot Training & Operations App

Skybound is a comprehensive app developed by Namastech to assist pilots with training, flight planning, progress tracking, and daily operational needs. Designed for both student pilots and seasoned aviators.

Key Features of Skybound

  • Interactive flight lessons and simulations
  • Real-time flight planning and weather integration
  • Progress tracking for training milestones
  • Community forums for pilot discussions
  • Maintenance logs and aircraft management
